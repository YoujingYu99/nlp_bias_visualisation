We are two Engineering students from the University of Cambridge. This is our summer project on visualising data bias. The aim is to create a dynamic data statement, with which users of the data can visualise the potential biases in a sample text or corpora in an interactive and informative way. Defined as the “use of computer-supported, interactive, visual representations of data to amplify cognition, or the acquisition and use of knowledge”, interactive visualisation enables users to actively control the kind of information and details they want to look for and how they are represented in the data.
